# WiFi / Evil Portal Attacks (AU Focus)

## High Value Targets in Australia
- Telstra, Optus, Vodafone, TPG, Aussie Broadband captive portals
- NBN connection troubleshooting pages
- Hotel / motel / caravan park guest WiFi
- Public "free WiFi" at shopping centres, airports, pubs
- Corporate guest networks

## Current Reality (2026)
Many Australian ISPs still have relatively weak captive portal implementations. Good Evil Portal + good social engineering (fake "NBN outage" or "speed test" pages) still works on a surprising number of people.

## Recommended Approach
- Use good-looking cloned login pages for major AU providers
- Combine with QR codes or "technician" pretext
- Flipper + WiFi dev board or ESP32-based solutions
