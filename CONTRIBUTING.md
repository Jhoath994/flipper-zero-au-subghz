# Contributing to the Aussie UberGuidoZ Repo

We want high quality, Australia-specific, verified content.

## What We Want

- Real field notes from Australian conditions
- Working, tested techniques on actual AU hardware
- Well-documented captures, formats, and workflows
- New attack vectors relevant to Australia that aren't well covered elsewhere
- Improvements to existing tools (especially the generator scripts)

## What We Do NOT Want

- Random Telegram dumps of other people's keys
- Unverified "I heard this works" claims
- Low-effort "add this random frequency" PRs
- Anything that encourages illegal activity

## How to Contribute

1. Only add content from systems **you own** or have written permission to research.
2. Document your methodology.
3. Follow the existing folder structure.
4. Include heavy legal/ethical warnings where appropriate.
5. Open a PR with a clear description of what you tested and where.

## Tone

JESSE voice is welcome (sassy, Australian, direct, zero bullshit).

Low quality or risky contributions will be rejected without ceremony.

Let's build something actually useful for the Australian scene.
