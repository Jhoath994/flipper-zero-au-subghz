# Ethics & Legal (Australia) - Read This

This repo exists because knowledge of how these systems work helps people secure their own property and conduct legitimate red team / physical security assessments.

It does **not** exist so you can be a cunt.

## Australian Legal Reality (2026)

- Using radio devices (Sub-GHz, RFID, etc.) to interfere with or access systems you do not own is illegal under the Radiocommunications Act and Criminal Code.
- Cloning access credentials (iButton, RFID, NFC) without authorisation is theft-related offending in every state.
- BadUSB / phishing payloads used without consent = computer offences.
- Setting up Evil Portals on public or third-party networks = illegal.

Penalties can include fines and jail time. "I saw it on GitHub" is not a defence.

## Red Lines

- Never use anything in this repo on systems, vehicles, or networks that do not belong to you without **written** authorisation.
- Never share working clones of other people's keys/cards/fobs.
- Never help randoms on the internet break into shit.
- If someone asks you to "just test this gate for me" and it's not yours — the answer is always no.

## Good Use Cases

- Testing and hardening your own vehicles, gates, workshop, and home.
- Authorised physical security assessments with a clear scope and sign-off.
- Research and education (with ethics approval where required).
- Defensive work: understanding how easy certain systems are to attack so you can recommend better ones.

## JESSE's Rule

If you have to ask "is this legal?", the answer is almost always "no, don't do it".

Do the right thing or get the fuck out of the community.
