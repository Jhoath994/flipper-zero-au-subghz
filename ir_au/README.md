# Infrared (IR) - Australian Focus

## Still Relevant In Australia
- Many older garage doors and gates (especially pre-2015) use simple IR remotes.
- Air conditioners (especially in older rentals and regional Australia).
- Some TVs, projectors, and AV equipment in pubs/clubs.
- Older car central locking / boot releases (rare but exists).

## Flipper IR Capabilities
Excellent. Can record, replay, and brute-force many simple IR protocols.

## This Folder
For AU-specific IR remote captures, aircon codes that are common in Australia, and notes on which older gate systems are still IR-based.

Low priority compared to Sub-GHz / RFID / iButton, but worth documenting when you find them.
